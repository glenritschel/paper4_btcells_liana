## Key Finding

Negative correlation between B cell activation and pro-fibrotic T cell programs:
- B activation vs Th2: Spearman r = -0.368, p = 2.089×10⁻¹⁰
- B activation vs Th17: Spearman r = -0.297, p = 3.995×10⁻⁷
- n = 280 patient samples

Mechanistic explanation: Galectin-9-TIM-3 immunoregulatory axis
